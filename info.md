# iRV32 Stereo

Home Assistant integration for iRV32V2 RV stereos over Bluetooth Low Energy.

Sends ATKEY GATT commands to control source, zones, playback, presets,
and volume. Works through ESP32 Bluetooth Proxies as well as the Home
Assistant host's local Bluetooth adapter.

See the [README](https://github.com/mcrw27/irv32) for the full ATKEY
reference and source-cycling notes.
